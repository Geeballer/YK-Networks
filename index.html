What we do:

We build digital tools and services

Our digital tools and services help businesses in the Nigerian informal markets grow, scale and create more value. We're building the ecosystem that will power digital commerce in the informal sector. Our target are our open markets traders, and we are building services and digital assets that make every trader, every small shop owner, every corner shop, every hustler digital-ready. YK Networks is an internet startup that is creating digital platforms for businesses in Nigeria's informal markets. Our digital tools and services are built on three pillars:

Seamless and Reliable Connectivity Borderless Payment Solutions Discoverability.

We grow businesses in Nigeria through internet-enabled services on the Cloud.
